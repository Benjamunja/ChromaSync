# ChromaSync Privacy Policy **Effective Date: January 2025** **Last Updated: December 2025** ## 1. Introduction Welcome to ChromaSync ("we," "our," "us," or "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our mobile application. By using ChromaSync, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App. ## 2. Information We Collect ### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly ChromaSync is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information: - **Media Files**: When you select photos for editing or capture photos using the camera, these files are accessed only for processing purposes. All processing occurs locally on your device. - **Custom Presets**: Any custom gradients, overlays, or filters you create are stored locally on your device using secure local storage (AsyncStorage). - **App Settings**: Your preferences (such as water depth units, export quality, language preference) are stored locally on your device. ### 2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect We are committed to your privacy. We do NOT collect: - Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, address) - Location data (GPS coordinates, precise location) - Contacts, calendar, or other personal data - Biometric data - Payment information (the App is free to use) - Your photos (they never leave your device) - Usage analytics or app activity tracking - Device identifiers for tracking purposes (except as required by AdMob) ### 2.3 Automatic Information Collection The App may automatically collect certain limited information necessary for functionality: - **Device Information**: Basic device information (model, operating system version) may be collected by third-party services (Google AdMob) for advertising purposes. - **App Version**: The App version may be displayed in settings for support purposes. ## 3. Third-Party Services and Data Collection ### 3.1 Google AdMob ChromaSync uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. This service may collect certain information to provide and improve advertising services: **Information Collected by AdMob:** - Device identifiers (advertising IDs such as IDFA on iOS and AAID on Android) - Device information (model, operating system, screen resolution) - General location data (country/region level, not precise location) - App usage data related to ad interactions (clicks, impressions) - IP address (anonymized) **App Tracking Transparency (iOS):** - On iOS 14.5 and later, ChromaSync requests permission to track your activity across apps and websites for personalized advertising - You can choose to allow or deny this permission when prompted - If you deny tracking permission, you will still see ads, but they may be less relevant to your interests - You can change this setting at any time in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking **How AdMob Uses This Information:** - To show you relevant advertisements - To measure ad performance - To prevent fraud and abuse - To comply with legal requirements **Your Control:** You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings: - **iOS**: Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking - **Android**: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization For more information about Google's data practices, please visit: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) ### 3.2 Expo Services ChromaSync is built using Expo, which may collect anonymous usage statistics and crash reports. This information is used solely to improve the Expo platform and does not include personal information. ## 4. How We Use Your Information ### 4.1 Local Processing All photo editing is performed **entirely on your device**. Your media files: - Are never uploaded to our servers - Are never transmitted over the internet - Are never stored in cloud services - Remain completely private and under your control ### 4.2 App Functionality We use locally stored information to: - Provide personalized app experience (custom presets, settings) - Remember your preferences (language, units, export quality) - Enable app features (grid planner, batch editing) ### 4.3 Advertising AdMob uses collected information to: - Display relevant advertisements - Measure advertising effectiveness - Prevent fraudulent activity ## 5. Data Storage and Security ### 5.1 Local Storage All your data is stored locally on your device: - **Media Files**: Stored in your device's photo library (you control this) - **Custom Presets**: Stored in secure local storage (AsyncStorage) - **App Settings**: Stored in secure local storage (AsyncStorage) - **Grid Planner Data**: Stored in secure local storage (AsyncStorage) ### 5.2 No Cloud Storage We do not: - Maintain servers that store your data - Use cloud storage services for your content - Sync your data across devices - Back up your data to external servers ### 5.3 Data Deletion - **Custom Presets**: Can be deleted through Settings > Clear Custom Presets - **Grid Planner**: Can be cleared through Settings > Clear Grid Planner - **All Data**: Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data ### 5.4 Security Measures We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data: - All processing occurs locally on your device - No data transmission to external servers (except ad-related data handled by Google AdMob) - Local storage uses secure, device-native storage mechanisms - No collection of sensitive personal information ## 6. Data Sharing and Disclosure ### 6.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not share your media files, custom presets, or personal data with anyone. ### 6.2 Third-Party Services As described in Section 3, Google AdMob may collect and use data for advertising purposes in accordance with their privacy policy. This is the only third-party service that collects data through our App. ### 6.3 Legal Requirements We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests, such as: - Court orders or subpoenas - Government investigations - Protection of our rights or safety - Prevention of fraud or abuse ## 7.
